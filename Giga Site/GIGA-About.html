Welcome to G.I.G.A.

We Make Games. Seriously.

The Guild of Independent Game Authors is a student-based club at the University of Windsor dedicated to making computer games. We work individually and as teams to develop our game programming skills.

We encourage any and all students to come in and contribute! We need artists, animators, musicians, story writers, voice actors, and yes, even programmers!
